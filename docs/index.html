How It Works

1

You post on Reddit

Business as usual. Post in any subreddit.

2

Extension checks automatically

30 seconds after posting, PostGhost reads Reddit's own internal removed_by_category field — the same signal Reddit uses to hide your post.

3

You see the truth

A badge appears on your post: LIVE or GHOST — plus WHY it was removed.

Why Posts Get Ghosted

Spam Filter

Low karma or new account. Reddit's automated filter removes your post before anyone sees it.

AutoMod Rules

Subreddit-specific rules. Certain words, link patterns, or account requirements trigger auto-removal.

Moderator Removal

A human mod removed your post. Sometimes justified, sometimes not — either way, Reddit doesn't tell you.

Karma / Age Gate

Many subreddits require minimum karma or account age. Your post is removed silently if you don't meet the threshold.
